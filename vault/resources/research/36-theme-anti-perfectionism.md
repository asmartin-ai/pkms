# 36 — Theme: Anti-perfectionism & survival design

**Why this matters for the build:** perfectionism/overengineering is your #2 struggle, and "building the system replaced using it" is the most-repeated abandonment cause in the entire corpus. Mark each: ✅ / ❌ / ❓.

*Reactions transferred verbatim from `exports/phase2-reading-bundle.md`, 2026-06-12. NOTE: #1 and #2 are bare ❌ with no comment — these reject two heavily-evidenced guardrails, so they need a sentence of elaboration at the G1 gate before the build commits either way (see [[decisions]] G1).*

1. **Cap the customization surface deliberately.** Every knob, plugin, and theme is a perfectionism trap; survivors picked tools partly *because* they couldn't tinker. Ship opinionated and finished-feeling ([[11-hn]] F1, F2; [[13-youtube]] F13; [[12-reddit]] L2-08).
   **React:** ❌
2. **Anchor every feature to a named context of use** (hoarder triage, job-search ops, project notes) — anything serving "PKM in general" is the avoidance surface, cut it ([[18-landscape]] LS10, LS9).
   **React:** ❌
3. **Plan for the pivot: novelty decay is the lifecycle.** Your own saves show ~2-year tool eras. Markdown substrate + regenerable index means future-you can rebuild the front-end endlessly without losing data — scratch the novelty itch at the view layer ([[11-hn]] F15; [[13-youtube]] F6; [[17-hoarder-mining]] CH14).
   **React:** ✅ - Soft agree.
4. **Lapse-and-restart is the normal lifecycle, not failure.** Fully functional after a 3-week gap, zero catch-up debt, no streaks, re-entry welcomed ([[12-reddit]] F7, F12, L2-09).
   **React:** ✅
5. **Rules live in the environment, not in documentation.** A conventions doc will be written and then violated; encode rules as defaults, templates, automation — make the right thing the laziest thing ([[15-barkley]] BK10).
   **React:** ✅
6. **Spec against the worst state:** every critical path (capture, find, resume) must work at 11pm with near-zero working memory; "in a deep low the system has to get dumber" ([[11-hn]] F17; [[14-github]] F6).
   **React:** ✅
7. **Shame is a design input.** No guilt language, no streaks, no red badges, briefings end with an invitation; the shame-freeze loop is the mechanism that breaks ADHD users, and low self-compassion measurably mediates worse outcomes ([[14-github]] F2, F3; [[16-academic]] MO8).
   **React:** ✅
8. **The scaffolding never comes off.** Prosthetic, not training wheels — continued reliance IS the system working; never frame a support as something to graduate from ([[15-barkley]] BK8; [[16-academic]] SC12).
   **React:** ✅
